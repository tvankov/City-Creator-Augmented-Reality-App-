## City Creator v0.9.1 ‚Äì Augmented Reality App

City Creator is an **Augmented Reality city visualization app** built with **Unreal Engine 4**.

The app allows users to place **buildings and street elements** directly into the real world using AR, move, rotate, scale, and delete objects, and explore future city concepts on-site.


![Augmented-Reality-App-Android-Todor-Vankov_01-1536x922](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1b1b49a6-8847-48d3-90d5-21e3daefffc7)


### Features

* AR-based city creation
* Place buildings and street elements in real environments
* Move, rotate, scale, and delete objects
* Combine multiple elements to create city layouts
* Ideal for visualization and discussion of urban projects
